Mobilni dizajn

Mobile design

U današnje vreme neophodno je da svaki website poseduje svoju mobilnu verziju. Responsive dizajnom prilagođavamo website veličini svakog mobilnog telefona ili tableta. Cilj je da korisnik stekne podjednako iskustvo na bilo kom uređaju na kom pregleda sajt.

Nowadays, it is necessary that every website has its own mobile version. Responsive design adapts the website to the size of each mobile phone or tablet. The goal is for the user to gain the same experience on any device on which they view the website.

Podizanje online prisustva postaje sve više obavezno u današnje vreme. Posedovanjem kvalitetnog website-a ne samo da ste prisutni na internetu, već imate priliku da se svima predstavite na unikatan način.

Building an online presence is becoming more and more mandatory nowadays. By having a quality website, you are not only visible on the Internet, but you have the opportunity to present yourself to everyone in a unique way.
